Los Angeles Robotics (LA Robotics) Financial Reports

The LARobotics fiscal year (FY) begins on July 1 and ends on June 30.

At various times, Los Angeles Robotics financial reports have included the following divisions:

  • FIRST LEGO League (FLL)
  • FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC)
  • Nelson Team Grants (NTG)
  • Southern California Regional Robotics Forum (SCRRF)
  • Other

Fiscal Year 2020: 07-01-2020 through 06-30-2021

The following reports are PDF files of financial reports generated from Quicken.
The Summary Report covers the three fiscal years between 07-01-2018 and 06-30-2021.
The overall reports for LARobotics are followed by the reports for FLL, NTG, SCRRF, and Other programs.
The Account Balances, Category (Event/Fund), Payee and Tag (income and expense class) reports cover the two fiscal years between 07-01-2019 and 06-30-2021.
Category by Tag and Transactions reports cover the 2020 fiscal year between 07-01-2020 and 06-30-2021.
The Totals Report includes the account balances by half year and the changes in account balances by half year and by full year between 06-30-20 and 06-30-2021.
