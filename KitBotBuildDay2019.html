2019 Kit Bot Build Day

From Kit to running Bot in one day!

This is an important event to help rookie teams get their FRC build season off to a good start, but veteran teams are also welcome. Mentors will be available to demonstrate, help, and teach. By the end of the day, each team will have built and be able to drive their own working robot base. Having a working Bot on day two of the build season is especially helpful for Rookie teams that have never designed actuators, grippers, lifters, drive trains, etc. before.

Orange County

Date: Sunday, January 6, 2019

Time: 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Host: FRC Team 4470: TiGears

Venue: Valencia High School

Address: 500 Bradford Ave, Placentia, CA 92870

Near: The intersection of the 57 and 91 Freeways

Parking: Free parking in school parking lot on Bradford Ave

Fee: $25.00 - payable at the event - helps cover costs

Bring:

  • ENTIRE Kit of Parts
  • Windows 7/8/10 laptop (if you have one)
  • Whatever tools you have
  • Loaner tools and computers available
  • Do NOT assemble kit or install software
