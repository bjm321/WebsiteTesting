6 LAUSD VEX IQ Leagues

If enough teams register, our goal is to restart the 10 continuing LAUSD leagues and start up to new LAUSD leagues .

Each new league should have 16-24 teams. Continuing leagues should have 20-36 teams. Each league needs at least 16 teams to qualify the top teams for State Championships.

All new schools are expected to send one or more coaches to attend a Coach Basic Training session. VEX IQ Super Kits will be used for the training.

Coaches that attended a Coach Basic Training session may also attend a Coach Advanced Training session, which is intended for second-year coaches.

All new schools are expected to send one or more coaches to attend a League Coach Orientation Session.

Leagues will meet after school (4:00-7:00 PM) at least four times between October and January:

  • September: Coach Training and Coach Orientations for new coaches.
  • October: Qualification Session 1: Practice Teamwork Matches (optional) followed by Qualification Teamwork Matches
  • November and December: Qualification Sessions 2-3: Qualification Teamwork Matches
  • January: Remote Judging and Championship Session 4: Robot Skills Matches followed by Finals Teamwork Matches and Awards
