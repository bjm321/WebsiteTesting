SCRRF Scholarships

Each year, the Southern California Regional Robotics Forum has awarded three one-year scholarships of $1000.00 to high school seniors, helping to enable their education in STEM studies. Each award is non-renewable, and the winners have been recognized at the Los Angeles Regional FRC Competition, held in Long Beach.

Starting in 2019, LA Robotics has decided to stop granting scholarships to individals, and will instead use suplus funds for robotics team grants. This will have a greater overall impact by benefiting more students. Information on team grants will be available soon.
