2017 Fall Workshops

Cancelled/Postponed: The 2017 fall workshops will not be held on November 4th due to the low number of registered teams.

We invite all FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC), FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) and VEX Robotics Competition (VRC) teams to participate, since much of the information on how to innovate, how to build a drive system, scholarships, project management, and many other topics can help team members for all programs.

Date: November 4, 2017

Time: 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM

Host: FRC Team 4 Element

Venue: High Tech High Los Angeles

Address: 17111 Victory Blvd, Lake Balboa, CA. 91406

Registration:

Register by adding or updating a row for your team on the Google Docs spreadsheet:
SCRRF 2017 Fall Workshops Registration

The registration fee is $100 per team.

Payment can be made by:
        Electronic check sent to SCRRF@LARobotics.org,
        Check payable to Los Angeles Robotics, or
        Credit Card (via PayPal). 

Please do not let finances keep your team away from this or any other SCRRF event. Fees charged are intended to cover event costs. Any extra fees collected help fund scholarships for high school robotics team members. Please contact Los Angeles Robotics Treasurer LeRoy Nelson at 310-937-7226 if you need financial help to attend.

No longer available.

Please include your Program (FRC, FTC or VRC), team number and your school name on the memo line of your electronic check.

To pay by check, write a check for the registration fee payable to Los Angeles Robotics and either bring it to the registration table at the event or mail it to:

        Los Angeles Robotics
        Attn: LeRoy Nelson, Treasurer

        Manhattan Beach, CA 90266-6335

Please include your Program (FRC, FTC or VRC), team number and your school name on the memo line of your check.
